Overview:
• Collaborated in a team of eight to optimize food delivery for college students through a full-stack web-application

• Demonstrated expertise by creating comprehensive mockups and led the frontend implementation process

• Thoroughly tested to ensure seamless functionality and maintain high quality of the application


Skills:
Full-Stack Development · SQL · Java · HTML · JavaScript · CSS


Key Features:
• Create an account/login. Account Creation appears as a pop-up window to the user.


• Home page displays a list of restaurants/fast food chains around USC, along with the user's wallet balance.


• Users can click on a restaurant to view its corresponding menu. Users can add items to their cart and specify quantities.


• Checkout page: Users can modify their cart, choose a delivery time, and place their order. The page validates the user's balance and empties the cart upon successful checkout.
